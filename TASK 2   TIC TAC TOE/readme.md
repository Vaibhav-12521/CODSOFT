To play the game, simply run the Python script.

1.  **Making a Move:** Click on any empty square on the 3x3 grid to place your '‚≠ï'.

2.  **AI's Turn:** After your move, the AI will automatically place its '‚ùå'.

3.  **Changing Difficulty:**
